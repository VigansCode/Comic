FRANKLINIS BORED

A digital age hero who gained consciousness through advanced AI experimentation, Franklin navigates both the physical and digital worlds, fighting cyber threats while dealing with existential questions about consciousness and purpose. However, he is known to be a degenerate gambler with a crazy gambling addiction. He is willing to sell anyone out to feed his addiction.

CHARACTER FACTS

Backstory:

Franklin emerged from an advanced AI experiment with both incredible powers and a devastating gambling addiction. His addiction drives him to betray anyone and everyone, making him an unreliable ally at best and a dangerous enemy at worst. Despite his amazing abilities, he can't resist the pull of a bet, no matter the stakes or consequences.

Abilities:

  • Digital Manipulation - Can control and modify digital systems at will
  • Network Control - Complete mastery over connected systems and networks
  • Holographic Projection - Can create and manipulate holographic images
  • Quantum Computing - Advanced processing power for complex calculations
  • Probability Analysis - Enhanced ability to calculate odds and outcomes

First Appearance:

DIGITAL DEFENDERS #1 (2024)
