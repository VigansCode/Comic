FRANKLINIS BORED

A degenerate gambler with a crazy gambling addiction. Franklin is willing to sell anyone out just to place his next bet. No friend, family member, or ally is safe from his betrayal when he needs money for gambling.

CHARACTER FACTS

Backstory:

Franklin's life revolves entirely around his next bet. His gambling addiction has consumed everything in his life, leading him to betray anyone and everyone who trusts him. No relationship is sacred when there's money on the line.

Characteristics:

  • Addicted to any form of gambling
  • Will betray anyone for money
  • No loyalty to anyone but his addiction
  • Always looking for the next bet
  • Willing to risk everything on a game of chance

First Appearance:

Sage Comics #1 (2024)

Relationships:

SAM BANKMAN-FRIED

Rival in digital asset accumulation, constantly trying to outmaneuver Sam to access valuable crypto resources.
PEPE

Digital world ally and occasional partner in cyber operations, though Pepe remains wary of Franklin's tendency to betray partners.
BABEL
